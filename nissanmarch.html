Nissan March/Micra (K10, K11)

The Nissan Micra, or Nissan March in Japan, is a popular supermini car produced by Nissan since 1982. Originally replacing the Nissan Cherry, it was exclusively sold through Nissan's Japanese dealership network until 1999. Compact and versatile, the Micra became Nissan's smallest vehicle until the introduction of kei cars in Japan.
