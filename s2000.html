Honda S2000

The Honda S2000, produced from 1999 to 2009, is a celebrated open-top sports car from Honda. Initially revealed as the SSM concept in 1995, it was launched in 1999 for Honda's 50th anniversary. The "S" stands for "sports," continuing Honda's tradition from the 1960s. The S2000 boasted a remarkable specific power output, making it highly sought after in the car community.
