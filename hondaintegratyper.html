Honda Integra Type R (DC5)

The DC5 Type R, exclusive to Japan, is praised for its lively engine and responsive handling. Sporting a standard K20A 2.0-liter DOHC i-VTEC engine, it delivers 220 horsepower (164 kW). The "i" in i-VTEC signifies intelligent VTEC, utilizing Variable Timing Control (VTC) for up to 50 degrees of timing advancement.
