Nissan Skyline (C10)

The third generation Skyline C10 was born in 1968 after a full-model change. It was famous for its nickname "Hakosuka (Boxy Skyline)". Features included wider and lower body and edgy styling. Especially the character line on the rear fender was designed based on the hydrodynamics and later called "Surfin' Line".
