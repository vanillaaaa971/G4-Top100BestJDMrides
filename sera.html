Toyota Sera

The Toyota Sera, known as model EXY10, is a unique 3-door hatchback coupe manufactured by Toyota from 1990 to 1996, exclusively for the Japanese market. It features distinctive butterfly doors and a glass roof canopy, giving it a futuristic appearance. The Sera debuted as the AXV-II concept car in 1988 and quickly gained attention for its innovative design, with the production version unveiled at the 1989 Tokyo Motor Show.
