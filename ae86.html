Toyota AE86 Sprinter Trueno/Corolla Levin

The AE86, also known as the Toyota Levin or Corolla GT, is a lightweight coupe cherished for its agile handling and drift-friendly traits. As the fourth generation of the Sprinter Trueno, it's renowned among enthusiasts for its nimble performance. The name "AE86" provides insight into its model specifics: "A" denotes the engine type ("4A"), "E" signifies the range (Corolla), "8" indicates the generation, and "6" denotes the model's variant.
