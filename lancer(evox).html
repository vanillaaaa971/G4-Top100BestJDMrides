Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution (Evo X)

The Lancer Evolution X is a high-performance sedan celebrated for its turbocharged engine and all-wheel-drive system, making it a favorite among rally enthusiasts. With a 1998 cc petrol engine and automatic transmission, it delivers a mileage of 15 kmpl and features a ground clearance of 140 mm. This 5-seater car comes in multiple variants with different fuel types, providing options to suit various preferences and driving needs.
