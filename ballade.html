Honda Ballade Sports CR-X

The Honda CR-X, originally known as the Honda Ballade Sports CR-X, is a front-wheel-drive sport compact car made by Honda from 1983 to 1991. It was marketed in some regions as the Honda Civic CRX. The CR-X offered a blend of economy and sportiness, with variants seating two or four passengers depending on the market. Known for its performance and fuel efficiency, it was succeeded by the Honda CR-X del Sol.
