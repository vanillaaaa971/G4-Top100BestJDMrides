Mazda Cosmo

The Mazda Cosmo is a stylish luxury grand tourer crafted by Mazda, renowned for its groundbreaking rotary engine technology and elegant design. The Series I/L10A Cosmo boasted a 0810 two-rotor engine, generating approximately 110 horsepower, with advanced features like a Hitachi four-barrel carburetor and a unique ignition system employing dual distributors and two spark plugs per chamber.
