Nissan Skyline GT-R (R34)

The Nissan Skyline GT-R (R34) is a legendary sports car known for its powerful twin-turbo engine and advanced technology. Produced from 1999 to 2002, it gained fame for its racing success in events like the Super GT series and the 24 Hours of Le Mans. With features like all-wheel drive and four-wheel steering, it set new standards for performance cars of its time. Loved by car enthusiasts, the R34 GT-R remains an iconic symbol of speed and innovation in the automotive world.
