Honda NSX (First Generation)

The NSX, a groundbreaking supercar by Honda, gained acclaim for its innovative engineering and exceptional handling. Originating from Honda's mid-1980s experimentation with a mid-engined setup in a modified Honda City, the NSX emerged as a rival to European counterparts in performance. Spearheaded by designers Nakano and Uehara, it signaled Honda's debut in the high-performance sports car market, setting new standards with its advanced technology and thrilling driving experience.
