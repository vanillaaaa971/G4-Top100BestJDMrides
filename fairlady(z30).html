Nissan Fairlady Z (S30)

The Fairlady Z, introduced in late 1969, is a classic sports car cherished for its sleek design and thrilling driving experience. Sporting a stylish coupe body, it featured a powerful L20 2.0-liter straight-six SOHC engine and rear-wheel drive configuration. With its engine derived from the Datsun 510's four-cylinder, it delivered 130 horsepower and came with a choice of four- or five-speed manual transmission.
