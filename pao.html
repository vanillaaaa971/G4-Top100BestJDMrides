Nissan Pao

The Nissan Pao is a retro-styled hatchback produced by Nissan from 1989 to 1991. It was exclusively sold in Japan at Nissan Cherry Stores. First unveiled at the 1987 Tokyo Motor Show, it gained popularity quickly, with over 51,000 orders placed within three months. Made by Nissan's Pike Factory, it's part of Nissan's "Pike cars" lineup, which includes the Figaro, Be-1, and S-Cargo.
