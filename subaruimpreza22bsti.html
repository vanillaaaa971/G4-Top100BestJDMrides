Subaru Impreza 22B STI

The 1998 Subaru Impreza 22B STI stands out as a limited-edition gem, crafted to commemorate Subaru's 40th anniversary and their triumphant streak in the FIA World Rally Championship. Sporting an eye-catching widebody design, this 2-door rally homologation special exudes aggressive styling and delivers blistering performance. With just 399 units produced for domestic markets, and an additional 25 for export, it quickly became a sought-after collector's item, selling out shortly after its release.
